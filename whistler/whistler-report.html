Alpine Forecast Synopsis

Updated February 05, 2018 at 7:07 AM

A ridge starts to slowly build over the eastern Pacific today, with some sporadic flurry activity continuing through the region as some weak instability will linger into tomorrow. Mild alpine temperatures will continue, with alpine inversions potentially setting up.

Mid-week one last system is expected to move through the region before the ridge more firmly establishes itself, bringing some sunshine into the province.

What to Wear

These heavy storms pick up moisture from the pacific and come catapulting towards the coast. Higher levels of moisture also present the biggest challenges for waterproof layers and accessories. Be sure to have your best GORE-TEX® layers on and maybe even a spare lens for your goggles.
